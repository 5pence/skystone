Hi, I'm Paul Devon Young

I work in the processing and making of clays, kilns, pigments including a theme of fossil-bone marble sculpture. I make a wide range of outdoor firing chambers for my modified clays.

I have some items for sale, as well as occasionally run workshops. I also take commissions. Please get in touch to find out more - I am looking forward to hearing from you.
